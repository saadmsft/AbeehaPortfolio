About Abeeha

A Fashion Design student at Pakistan Institute of Fashion & Design (PIFD), currently in sixth semester and working on mini-thesis. Core strengths lie in illustration rendering, textile development, and silhouette design.

Adapting to the technological landscape of 2026 with strong proficiency in digital tools such as Photoshop, Illustrator, Procreate, alongside a foundational command of CLO 3D. My work bridges traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology.
